Tempe

Tempe is a beautiful city in Arizona located just east of Phoenix. The city is home to Arizona State University. It features a lot of small "mom- and-pop shops" that have homemade meals prepared and ready to eat. If you ever visit Tempe I would advise you to stay indoors and avoid the scorching hot weather.
